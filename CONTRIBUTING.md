## Branch & PR Rules

- Do **not** push directly to `main`.
- All changes must be proposed via a **Pull Request**.
- Create a branch named after your change, e.g.:
  - `feat/escrow-lifecycle-diagram`
  - `fix/api-error-handling`
- Keep PRs focused and small whenever possible.
